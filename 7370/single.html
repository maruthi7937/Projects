University Housing Single Bedroom Apartments

Welcome to the University Housing Apartments, Residential Life's on-campus apartment housing. Please note that apartments are available only for enrolled MU students and working persons who meet one of the following criteria:

  1. Married students
  2. Students with children
  3. Single graduate students
  4. Anyone who is working for University
