University Housing 3BR Apartments

3 bedroom apartments in a quiet wooded setting with a great balcony view. A wide variety of floorplans are available, some with fireplaces and gas heat included. Our units feature large glass. Please note that apartments are available only for enrolled MU students and working persons who meet one of the following criteria:

  1. Married students
  2. Single graduate students
  3. Anyone who is working for University

Three BedRoom Details

In each 3 Bed Room Apartment up to 6 persons can leave. If we notice any extra person leaving without informing us then Residents of that apartment has vacate the house and penalty may be charged. Features includes: Floors: Carpet, Fireplace: Wood, Air Conditioning: WALL UNITS, Heat: Gas, Range: Electric, Bathroom Details: Combined Shower & Bathtub

Unique Features: Some have washer/dryer connections, some have walk-in closets, some have skylights, some have woodburning fireplaces, some have a choice between gas or electric heat
