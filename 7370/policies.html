Policies

Some of the policies for our Apartments are listed below. These policies are in accordance with the Department of Residential Life. For a full list policies, please refer to Residential Life Rules and Regulations in your Tiger Guide.

Moving Out

This contains the policies you should be aware of when moving out of your apartment. You must give notice if you plan to leave your apartment,return your apartment keys and clean your apartment to avoid cleaning charges. If you are ready to give us notice that you are leaving University Housing Apartments, fill out the checkout form.

Alcohol

Residents who are at least 21 years old may have alcohol within their apartment, as long as they don't disturb other residents.

Smoking

Residence are not allowed to smoke in our building. Our Apartments are smoke-free.

Pets

Fish are the only pets allowed at University Student Apartments.
