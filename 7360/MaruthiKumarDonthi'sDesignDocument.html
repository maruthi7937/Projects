Maruthi Kumar Donthi's Design Document on Online Window Shopping

Purpose:

The name of the website will be Online Window Shopping. Nowadays everyone accessing internet for buying and selling anything. This Window Shopping website encompasses the details of wide verity of Accessories. The website includes departments like Electronics, computers, Grocery, Food items gifts and etc. The main purpose of this site is to inform the customers about the latest models of accessories of different brands with the price and other information. The above website will help people to know the different models of accessories with the price and their availability details.

Audience:

The target audience for this web site will be people who love shopping. Irrespective of age, gender, location and language. People who is willing to buy something, They can use our website to know what all different types of products present in current market and price of those products.

Needs Assessment:

I asked 7 people from my intended target audience to give their opinions on what all should be included in this website. They wanted to include latest released products in our website. They also wanted the products to be divided into categories.

Questions asked to the target audience:

What type of information would you find most helpful on our web site?
  • All kinds of products with price and included good images of products.
  • Links to other websites offering additional information about Products.
  • All varieties of products with different colors of availability makes it easy to select products
  • Information of special discount offers of products.
What type of information you do not want to see included on the website?
  • Most said that they do not want to see irrelevant advertisements.
  • Most of them replied that they do not want to see Information not related to Products.
What kind of look you expect from this website?
  • It should have a colorful and attractive layout with proper navigational features.
  • Readability level of the content should be high.
  • Images should be attractive. It would be better if they have high resolution.
Do you have any suggestions ?
  • Few of them were suggested to include demo videos of products.
  • Few people suggested to categories the products on the basis of brand.

Content:

Based on my ideas and output of the target audience, I have chosen to include the following content areas

  • Home Page: It will contain a welcome statement, Logo of website and contact information. It will also contain links to all the supporting pages of the website.
  • Computers: This page includes Desktops, laptops and computer related accessories that are categorized into different groups based on brands like Dell, Lenovo, Apple and etc
  • TV and Home appliances: This page includes details of tv and home appliances like washers and dryers, refrigerators, microwaves and other.
  • Mobile phones: This page includes details of all mobile phones.
  • Gifts and toys: This page includes gifts and details of toys.
  • Grocery: This page includes details of groceries like beverages, food, baking and etc.
  • Cameras: This page includes details of cameras and watches.

Presentation of Information/Rationale of Organization:

  • To provide a consistent look and feel across all pages and to give my audience a sense of connected fluidity in my site, all pages will contain / use
    • The same banner logo at the top of each page.
    • A heading just below the top horizontal navigation that will provide the page name.
    • Same navigation conventions, same heading formats and same colors are used in all pages.
    • Contact information at the bottom of each page.
    • Link back to the home page.
  • The home page
    • It will be laid out in a sequential format.
    • It will provide links to the main sections.
    • It will also contain a welcome statement, the mission statement and contact information.
  • All subsequent pages will provide horizontal navigation (text and in some cases graphics also) across both the top and bottom of the page.
  • I know I'll have enough information on each page to cause my audience to scroll down so I want them to have access to the navigation menu at the bottom of the page too.
  • The Computers page will have information that is sub-divided into 4 sections i.e. Lenovo, Dell, Apple, Samsung. It will have all details of computers with price and configuration are provided in tabular manner to make it easy for the audience.
  • The TV and Home Appliance page will have information that is sub-divided into 4 sections i.e. TV, Refrigerators, Microwaves, Washers and Dryers. It will have images of all products and price provided in table to make it easy for the audience.
  • The Mobile phones page will have information of mobile phones which are divided into 4 groups i.e Iphone, Samsung, HTC, Blackberry.
  • The Gift and Toys page will have information categories into 4 sections i.e. Flowers, Jewelry, Toys, Greeting cards. These products details are provided in bulleted list to make it easy for the audience.
  • The grocery page will have details of Food, Baking, Beverages, Spices. This information is provided in bulleted list to make it easy for the audience.
  • The Cameras page contains details of different types of cameras.

Media:

Nearly all the multimedia files like images, audio, video files will be taken from internet. I will give due credit to the sources of the files. I will only be incorporating the freely available files over internet. Also there will be a disclaimer in home page such as "The multimedia elements used in the website do not belong to me and they have been taken from different sources with proper credit given to the sources at appropriate places in this website. Some of the images which are copyrighted are clearly marked with copy; in the caption. User is advised to take permission from the relevant sources." I will also be creating my own logo using software paint.

Color scheme and rationale:

Since most of Online Shopping web sites using light background colors .I would like the background to be in the light colors (gray). The other colors used would be black (for text ) and blue (for headings).So I am going to use these colors in my final website. I might be using all these colors to create my logo.
