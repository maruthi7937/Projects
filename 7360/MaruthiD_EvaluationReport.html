Recommended/Planned Changes

Enriching content

  • More content: I would like to add more content for product desription by adding discount offers information.

Reflections on Evaluation Process

I am really happy after I performed this evaluation process. It was useful in various ways- the first and most important thing I go to notice the perception of different people about my website. It also helped me to get an overview of how target users understand the website and content in it. Also by performing evaluation, the four of them gave me suggestions to make improvements, for which I thank them. Also, all the evaluator's different viewpoints in their mind has helped me understand how different each person would see a page.

Finally I would like to conclude by saying that this evaluation has helped me a lot by different suggestions, changes provided by the evaluators. This makes site more robust and as suggested by my evaluators, I will add more media like images and also the power point presentation. I will make changes keeping in mind a casual visitor's perspective.
